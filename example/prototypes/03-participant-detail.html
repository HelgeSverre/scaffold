About this Screen

What is this screen for?

The Participant Detail screen shows everything about a single person's relationship with the festival. View all their entitlements, credentials, scan history, and activity in one place.

Workflow Coverage

This screen covers the Participant Onboarding, Meal Voucher Lifecycle, and Equipment Check-Out workflows from the proposal. It is the central hub for managing a participant's festival experience.

Key Interactions

  • Entitlements tab: View all assigned items with their current state, source (rule or manual), and usage. Add new entitlements or revoke existing ones.
  • Credentials tab: Manage physical/digital tokens (QR codes, badges). Issue new credentials or replace lost ones.
  • Scan History: Complete audit trail of every scan for this participant.
  • Activity Log: System events showing rule evaluations, manual changes, and credential issuance.
  • Entitlements created by rules show a "Rule" tag. Manually overridden ones show an "Overridden" warning badge.
  • The sidebar shows current zone location and quick stats.
