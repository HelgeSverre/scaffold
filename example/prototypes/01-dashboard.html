About this Screen

What is this screen for?

The Dashboard provides a real-time overview of your festival's entitlement system. Monitor scan activity, entitlement distribution, zone occupancy, and rule engine status. This is the first screen administrators see when managing accreditation and entitlements.

Workflow Coverage

This screen provides a summary view of all workflows from the proposal: Participant Onboarding, Access Control, Meal Distribution, Equipment Check-Out, and Zone Management.

Key Interactions

  • View stat cards for quick metrics overview
  • Monitor scan activity chart over the past 7 days
  • Track recent scans in real-time
  • Review entitlement summary by category
  • Monitor rule engine status and pending evaluations
  • Check zone occupancy and capacity alerts
